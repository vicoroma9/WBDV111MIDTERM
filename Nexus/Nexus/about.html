About Nexus

NEXUS is a premier technology event that unites students, developers, and tech enthusiasts to explore the cutting edge of IT and robotics. Through hackathons, competitions, showcases, and interactive exhibits, participants get the chance to innovate, collaborate, and bring futuristic ideas to life. NEXUS inspires the next generation of innovators to learn, create, and shape the future of technology.
